Solano Elementary School | Osborn School District

"Open Fields Open Play" at Solano Elementary School is a shared use project dedicated to opening our play spaces and giving back to the community we serve. We believe everyone should have a safe place to come and share with their families while meeting other community members.

The area has two large soccer fields on baseball field a volleyball area, two basketball courts, and an outdoor workout equipment area.

Who we are
